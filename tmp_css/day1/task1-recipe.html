Classic Pomodoro Pasta

A warm, simple Italian favorite ready in 20 minutes.

Serves: 2

Time: 20 mins

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • Salt & pepper
  • Fresh basil & Parmesan

Method

Cook the spaghetti in salted water until al dente.

Warm olive oil, sauté garlic, then add tomato puree.

Season, toss with pasta, and finish with basil and Parmesan.
