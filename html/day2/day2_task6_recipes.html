Italian Pasta Dishes

Ingredients

  • Pasta (spaghetti, penne)
  • Tomatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
    • Substitute: roasted garlic paste
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in salted water.
  2. Prepare sauce with tomatoes, garlic, and olive oil.
  3. Combine pasta and sauce, garnish with basil.
